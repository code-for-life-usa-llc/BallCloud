Cade Cunningham

Cunningham attended Bowie High School in Arlington. Soon into his freshman season, he became a starter on the varsity team.Cunningham averaged 15.2 points, 6.4 rebounds and three assists per game, helping Bowie reach the District 6A Region I final. In December 2017, early in his sophomore season, he suffered an injury while attempting to dunk at a tournament in Houston.Cunningham finished the season averaging 18.8 points, 8.2 rebounds, and 5.3 assists per game.

Cade Cunningham is a Point Guard from Bowie High school at the height of 6' 7" and a weight of 215 lbs.
